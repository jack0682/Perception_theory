## Why this structure

Single-direction promotion pipeline to keep the theory from being contaminated:

```
logs/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md   (raw, this file)
      ↓ topic reorganization
working/<topic>.md          (active theory work)
      ↓ proof + review
canonical/canonical.md      (authoritative — no return path)
```

canonical/ accepts promoted content only. Retractions stay explicit. working/ holds everything in progress. logs/ holds time-ordered raw traces.
