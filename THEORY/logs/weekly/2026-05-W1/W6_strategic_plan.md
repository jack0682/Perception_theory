## 4.2 G2 [P0 MUST] NQ-L1M-2 CSEH 2007 Factor-2 Sharpness

### 4.2.1 Goal statement

L-M-2 В§5.4м—җм„ң мӮ¬мҡ©н•ң bottleneck-stability factor-2 ($|\ell_i - \ell_i^{(u^{(j)})}| \le 2 \cdot \rho_{\mathrm{pert}}/2 = \rho_{\mathrm{pert}}$)к°Җ sharpмқём§Җ м җкІҖ + к°ҖлҠҘ мӢң tighter bound лҸ„м¶ң.

### 4.2.2 Mathematical depth

**Question**: CSEH 2007 Theorem 4.2 ($W_\infty(\mathrm{Dgm}(f), \mathrm{Dgm}(g)) \le \|f - g\|_\infty$)м—җм„ң individual barмқҳ length ліҖнҷ”лҠ” м–ҙл–»кІҢ bounded?

**Standard analysis**: bar $[d_i, b_i]$к°Җ perturbation нӣ„ $[d_i', b_i']$лЎң ліҖкІҪ; bottleneck matchingмқҙ $|b_i - b_i'| \le \delta$ + $|d_i - d_i'| \le \delta$ where $\delta = W_\infty$. л”°лқјм„ң $|\ell_i - \ell_i'| = |(b_i - d_i) - (b_i' - d_i')| \le |b_i - b_i'| + |d_i - d_i'| \le 2\delta$.

**Sharpness м җкІҖ**: мң„мқҳ $|\ell_i - \ell_i'| \le 2\delta$лҠ” birthмҷҖ deathк°Җ *л°ҳлҢҖ л°©н–Ҙ*мңјлЎң shift мӢң sharp. terminal-death convention н•ҳм—җм„ңлҠ” $d_i = d_i' = 0$ (terminal-death survivors), мҰү $|d_i - d_i'| = 0$. мқҙ кІҪмҡ° $|\ell_i - \ell_i'| = |b_i - b_i'| \le \delta$ вҖ” **factor 2к°Җ 1лЎң мӨ„м–ҙл“Ұ**.

**Implication**: Type-N bars under terminal-death conventionм—җм„ң factor-2 вҶ’ 1 sharpening к°ҖлҠҘ. мқҙлЎң мқён•ҙ В§5.4мқҳ boundк°Җ $\ell_i \le \ell_{\min} - 3\rho_{\mathrm{pert}} + \rho_{\mathrm{pert}}/2 = \ell_{\min} - 5\rho_{\mathrm{pert}}/2$лЎң tighten. л”°лқјм„ң $\tau_* = \min(5\rho_{\mathrm{pert}}/2, \rho_{\mathrm{res}}, r_{\mathrm{birth}})$ вҖ” 25% нҷ•мһҘ.

### 4.2.3 Subtleties

- **Terminal-deathмқҳ м •нҷ•н•ң м •мқҳ**: T-L1-Fмқҳ P0 ("terminal-death $H_0$ superlevel persistence convention")к°Җ лӘЁл“  barsмқҳ deathлҘј 0мңјлЎң л‘җлҠ”м§Җ, лҳҗлҠ” мқјл¶Җл§Ң (terminal survivorsл§Ң) л‘җлҠ”м§Җ м •нҷ•нһҲ нҷ•мқё.
