**Problem:** K=2 stability is vacuous without external per-formation mass constraint  
**Evidence:** exp62, exp63 show K=2 energy is ~50% more expensive than K=1  
**Impact:** All K-field theorems depend on "fixed K" assumption (unresolved)  
**Status:** Documented in assumption_registry.md (A-0012)  
**Location:** open_problems.md (OP-0001)

### M-1: K=1 Always Preferred 🔴 CRITICAL

**Problem:** Energy landscape monotonically prefers K=1  
**Evidence:** Direct calculation + empirical confirmation (exp62, exp63, exp71–exp73)  
**Impact:** No mechanism for K>1 emergence from energy minimization alone  
**Status:** Documented in assumption_registry.md (A-0013)  
**Location:** open_problems.md (OP-0002)

### MO-1: Morse Theory Inapplicable 🔴 CRITICAL

**Problem:** Constrained manifold Σ²_M has corners; smooth Morse theory invalid  
**Evidence:** Manifold geometry analysis  
**Impact:** Global optimization analysis on K=2 landscape incomplete  
**Status:** Documented in assumption_registry.md (A-0015)  
**Location:** open_problems.md (OP-0003)

- ✅ Explicitly documented (not hidden)
- ✅ Registered as critical open problems (OP-0001/0002/0003)
- ✅ Included in archival records (for audit trail)
