# Claims Registry — 06_claims/

Central registry of all substantive claims made by the theory, with validation status and supporting evidence.

## Purpose

Document and track:
- Mathematical claims (theorems, lemmas, propositions)
- Empirical claims (experimental predictions, observed phenomena)
- Conceptual claims (interpretations, relationships)

All claims include confidence level and validation status.

## Structure

Each claim is assigned: `C-XXXX` where XXXX is zero-padded.

**Format:** See `99_templates/TEMPLATE_claim.md`

**Metadata:**
- `category`: mathematical | empirical | conceptual
- `status`: proposed | validated | rejected | conditional
- `confidence`: 0-100%
