# Questions Registry — 05_questions/

Central registry of all open questions, research gaps, and foundational inquiries across the SCC theory.

## Purpose

Track all unresolved questions at every level:
- Foundational conceptual questions (what is cohesion?)
- Technical implementation questions (how do we compute X?)
- Methodological questions (how do we validate Y?)
- Open problems blocking progress (F-1, M-1, MO-1)

## Structure

Each question is assigned a unique ID: `Q-XXXX` where XXXX is a zero-padded number.

**Format:** See `99_templates/TEMPLATE_question.md`

**Metadata:**
- `category`: foundational | technical | methodological | open_problem
- `status`: open | resolved | blocked | delegated
- `priority`: critical | high | medium | low
